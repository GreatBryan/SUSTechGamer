In 190 A.D. (the first year of Chu Ping, Emperor Xian of the Han Dynasty). China is in real trouble. The Eastern Han Dynasty was divided into two parts. The weak Emperor Xian is just a puppet in the hands of warlord Dong Zhuo. This man's rule was savage and violent. With the growth of Dong Zhuo's power, the country gradually slipped into a hell that would never be destroyed. However, hope is also booming. As early as the rise of the yellow scarf rebellion, three heroes became brothers of different surnames, determined to face the trial of fate.
